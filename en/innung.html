We offer you the following benefits

19 benefits for guild members — at a glance in four areas

LEGAL & ADVICE
  • Up-to-date legal information — new laws, regulations and accident prevention rules
  • Free initial telephone advice on employment law from our association lawyer
  • Free telephone advice on collective wage questions
  • Representation of interests before ministries, authorities and municipalities
  • Contract templates such as employment and purchase contracts in the member area
SAVINGS & CONDITIONS
  • 50 % discount through preferential rates for specialist seminars
  • 75 % fee reduction for occupational health and safety support (FFK)
  • 20 % reduced GEMA licence rates
  • Special agreements with insurers — business liability, retirement provision and more
  • Favourable prices from electricity and gas suppliers
  • Framework agreements e.g. for new cars, books, linen and hotels
KNOWLEDGE & TRENDS
  • Important trade news in the free guild newsletter
  • Twice a year: hairstyle fashion and cosmetics trends at the HAIR FASHION PREVIEWS
  • Exclusive professional insights through the central association’s fashion magazines
  • Professional support from expert groups in the guild and trade association
COMMUNITY & REPRESENTATION
  • Delegates sent to trade associations, social insurance bodies and committees
  • Organisation of competitions and trade events
  • Trade-related public relations and joint advertising
  • Monitoring fair competition
