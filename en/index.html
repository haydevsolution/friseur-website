What the guild stands for

03 VALUES
i.

Inspiration

“Die Friseurinnung” is inspiring — we stand for unique ideas and rewarding impulses. This strengthens our community and the success of each individual member.

ii.

Excellence

We stand for quality and professionalism. Top performance is a standard we demand of ourselves — which is how we keep setting new benchmarks.

iii.

Community

Collaboration and mutual support are at our core. We are a community: we pull together and stand by each other with advice and action.
